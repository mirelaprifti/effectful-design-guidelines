Best Practices

  • Match nested radii. Inner elements should use a smaller radius than their container.
  • Use rounded-full sparingly. Reserve full rounding for avatars, badges, pills, and toggle controls.
  • Be consistent within groups. All buttons in a button group should share the same radius.
  • Consider the content. Larger containers benefit from larger radii; small, dense UI elements work better with smaller radii.
  • Avoid mixing too many values. Stick to 2-3 radius values per component to maintain visual consistency.
